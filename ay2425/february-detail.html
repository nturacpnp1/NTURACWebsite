February Events Highlight

Janitor's Appreciation Project 2.0

On 8th February, the Community Service Division collaborated with the NTU Muslim Society to organise Janitor's Appreciation Project 2.0 to show our appreciation to the janitors of NTU who worked hard to help keep the campus clean. Our volunteers interacted with the janitors through fun station games and a shared meal, creating a memorable and meaningful experience for everyone.


Networking Dinner

On 23rd February, the Club Service Division and Professional Development Division organised the Networking Dinner event. The attendees listened to insightful discussions with inspiring panellists, built meaningful and lasting connections, exchanged perspectives, and expanded their professional and social networks.


World Rotaract Week Carnival

On 24th and 25th February, the World Rotaract Week Division hosted their annual 2-day carnival event featuring divisional booths, fun and exciting games, lively photobooth, and amazing performances. The event ended with a successful food donation drive as well as a thrilling lucky draw session.


MINDS Outdoor Adventure (MOA): S.E.A. Aquarium

On 28th February. the Special Projects Division organised the MINDS Outdoor Adventure (MOA): S.E.A. Aquarium event. Our volunteers helped to bring smiles and joy to our MINDS beneficiaries, by guiding them through an amazing underwater journey at the S.E.A. Aquarium. It was a day full of adventure and unforgettable memories.
