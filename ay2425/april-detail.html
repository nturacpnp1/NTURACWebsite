March Events Highlight

Photography Workshop

On 11th April, the Youth Development Divison and the Publicity and Publication Division organised a photography workshop. Our volunteers guided the children to decorate their own polaroid holders and create a memorable experience for them.


Appreciation Night

On 13th April, the Club Service Division organised Rotaract Club of NTU's annual Appreciation Night event to express our gratitude to our beloved club members who have worked tirelessly throughtout the academic year and to appreciate their contributions towards the club.
